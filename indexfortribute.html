Tribute Page To Didier Drogba


Biography

Some legends are born, some are made and some make themselves. Didier Yves Drogba Tébily or Didier Drogba, as football fans know him around the world, belongs to the latter category.

The Ivory Coast striker is best known for his time at Chelsea. After arriving at Stamford Bridge from Marseille in the summer of 2004 for a then club record £24 million, he went on to help Chelsea win their first league title in 50 years. Drogba thrived at Chelsea and went on to establish himself as one of the most feared strikers in the EPL and the world.

“I believe a lot in destiny and I think that if something didn't happen, it was for a reason.” – Drogba
